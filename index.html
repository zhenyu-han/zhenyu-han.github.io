Current Research

Synthesis and Biological Testing of Quinone Products with Potential Anticancer Activity

Many of organic, natural compounds are isolated from plants and microorganisms and have been found to be useful in therapeutic applications. In the field of anti-cancer research, a wide range of natural products have been reported to have significant inhibitory activity. We aim to synthesize targed quinone products and test their anticancer activity in collaboration with University of Frankfurt in Germany.
