Research Interests

Overview

My research interests lie in bioorganic/medicinal chemistry. Previously I have worked in Professor Doug Young's Biochemistry lab, primarily involved in organic synthesis and unnatural amino acids, and I loved how chemistry and biology can work together. I also worked with Professor Dana Lashley on synthesis of quinone products to test anti-cancer effects. I hope that my  research will have biological or medicinal outcomes and implications by employing the tools of organic synthesis and analytical chemistry.
