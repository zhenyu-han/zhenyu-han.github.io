About


I was born in Hangzhou, one of the seven ancient capitals in China. I am currently studying at the College of William & Mary and expect to graduate with a Bachelor of Science degree in Chemistry in May, 2019.

I enjoy discovering the unknown by learning and applying my scientifc skills and knowledge through experimentation. My research interest lies in the interface between biochemistry and organic chemistry. I am also interested in employing theoretical/computational chemistry to shed light on experimental observations. I hope that my research will not only contribute to the current scientific understandings, but also benefit the society in the long run. You can read more about my research projects here.
