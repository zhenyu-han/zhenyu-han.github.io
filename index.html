About


I was born in Hangzhou, one of the seven ancient capitals in China. I am currently studying in the College of William & Mary and expect to graduate with a Bachelor of Science degree in Chemistry in May, 2019.

My research interest lies in the interface among biochemistry, organic chemistry, and computational chemistry, shaped by three college research experiences and my simple curiosity to the Nature. I enjoy discovering the unknown by learning and applying new skills and knowledge in the lab. In honing my scientific skills, I hope not only to contribute to the current scientific knowledge, but also to benefit the greater communities and society. You can read more about my research here.
